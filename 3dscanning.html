3D scanning and printing

I decided to make a dog bowl since I have a dog named Máni. I found a video on youtube which helped me. The first step was to Insert an image and attach it as a canvas. Then I had to enter all the dimensions as shown on the photo here below we did that by editing the canvas. As shown on the photo I put the center origin on the left bottom off the photo.

To make the corners i went to sketch and fillet and I did that for the four corners. And finally i went to sketch and revolve, I selected the far right side as the axis, the direction was One side and the opperation was New component.

The last part was to make the shell under the bowl, I went to modify shell and the inside thickenss was 2.5mm. Here bellow you can see the final sketch off the dog bowl. The first image is the upper part of the bowl.

The next image is the bottom part of the bowl.

Then I extruded the file as an stl file and opened it in Cura.

Then I put it to a usb and sent it to 3D printing this was the final result :
